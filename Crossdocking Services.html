Cross Docking and Warehousing Services throughout North America

Make sure your Supply Chain remains uninterrupted by incorporating Cross Docking and Warehousing Services.

Distribution, Warehousing, or Cross Docking, RJ Logistics has you covered

RJ Logistics has over 120,000 Sq. Ft. of warehousing space located halfway between the Ambassador and Blue Water Bridge in Michigan, Laredo International Bridge in Texas, and throughout Central Mexico to assist with our customers’ needs. Our team is trained and experienced with handling the storage, loading, unloading, and redistribution of goods from one facility to another. Our warehouses are monitored and secured 24/7 for the guaranteed safety of your commodities and our people. If you’re looking for reliable Warehousing and Cross Docking services, you can count on RJ Logistics.

Loading Docks present at all our terminals

Loading docks are available at all our terminals to manage the transfer of goods from one truck to another. Our mission is to make sure that your supply chain never comes to a halt due to common obstacles that most shippers face. If you are ahead of schedule and need temporary storage, we cover that too!

One-Stop-Shop for all your standard or specialized Cross-Docking needs

  • Licensed Forklift drivers on site
  • Overhead Crane Operators available upon request
  • After hours Cross-docking and Warehousing services available upon request
  • 24/7 Surveillance and Security at all our storage facilities.
